Culture

São Paulo is a concrete jungle with a heart of gold. Culture-wise, it’s a chaotic masterpiece that never sleeps. It’s the kind of place where high-end museums like MASP—with its iconic red pillars and massive art collection—sit alongside the vibrant, ever-changing street art of Beco do Batman in Vila Madalena. The city's energy is a relentless, 24/7 pulse, hosting massive global events like the São Paulo Art Biennial and the second-largest Gay Pride Parade in the world. This "city of all peoples" is a melting pot where you’ll hear a cocktail of languages and find creative subcultures reclaiming old industrial warehouses for techno parties and art galleries.

Food

When it comes to food, forget your diet; the city is the gastronomic capital of Latin America. You can start at the historic Municipal Market, or "Mercadão", to tackle a legendary mortadella sandwich—a 300g mountain of meat and melted cheese that’s been a local staple since a grumpy chef overstuffed one in the 1930s. For a different vibe, head to Liberdade, home to the largest Japanese community outside of Japan, where you can find authentic ramen that rivals anything in Tokyo. Whether it’s a Sunday pastel with sugarcane juice at a street fair or a late-night slice from one of the city’s 6,000 pizzerias, the culinary scene is an endless, delicious adventure.

Architecture

The architecture is a wild, soaring mix of "Old World" elegance and bold Modernism. Walking down Paulista Avenue feels like being in the centre of the world, surrounded by glass towers and the stark, "Paulista Brutalism" of architects like Paulo Mendes da Rocha, who used heavy concrete and geometric lines to redefine the city’s skyline. You can’t miss the curvy, tilde-shaped Copan Building designed by the legendary Oscar Niemeyer, a massive structure so large it has its own postal code. From the 19th-century European charm of the Municipal Theatre to the grey, imposing skyscrapers that stretch as far as the eye can see, the city is a beautiful, messy testament to human ambition.
